⚠ Verification Protocol

Standing rules for all searches and applications. Claude uses these in every session — they are binding instructions, not suggestions.

✓ Internal Consistency Rule — Standing Instruction
Core principle
Every item must tell one consistent story. When a role's status changes, ALL of the following must be updated together: the checklist item visual state, the deadline badge, the meta note, the ROWS status field, and the item's position (active tier vs archive). Contradictory signals in a single item are always an error to be corrected immediately.
Signal pairs that must never coexist in the same item
Never combine these… …with these "Confirmed live" / "Posted today" / "Active" "Confirmed closed" / "Not in portal" / "Do not apply" / "False positive" Active color badge (🔴🟡🟠🟢) Line-through text OR opacity:0.4 struck style ROWS status "Open" Item in archive section OR title says "REMOVED/ELIMINATED" ROWS status "Eliminated" or "AVOID" Item in active Tier 1–3 with open-color badge Tier 1 placement ⚠️ VERIFY IN PORTAL badge (unverified roles go to Tier 2 minimum) Date/freshness claim (e.g. "3 days ago") Source is aggregator only — must add VERIFY badge
Checklist for every status update
When any role is updated (confirmed open, closed, applied, or moved): (1) update the deadline badge text and color class; (2) update the meta note to remove outdated claims; (3) update the ROWS status field; (4) if closing — strike the item, add ❌ badge, move to archive, remove active color badge; (5) if confirming open — remove VERIFY badge, update to confirmed source, move to correct tier if needed.

⚠️ Verification Protocol — Standing Instructions
Core rule
Aggregator timestamps are never ground truth. The only verification that counts is the employer's own ATS — Workday, Oracle Cloud, governmentjobs.com, or the employer's direct careers portal. A role is confirmed open only when it appears in a live portal search on the day of the session.
Known false-positive patterns (do not trust these signals alone)
Jobright "X hours ago" Re-indexes stale cached listings with fresh timestamps. Confirmed Apr 17: "13h ago · <25 applicants" for VUMC Effort Reporting returned zero results in live Workday. Always verify in Workday before treating as fresh. governmentjobs.com /newprint/ Returns full posting content for any job ID — open or closed. MDHA Financial Analyst (/4805165) showed Position Closing Date Feb 7, 2025; MDHA P&D Analyst (/4777438) closed ~Oct 2024. Never use newprint URL as confirmation of open status. Aggregator "3–4 days fresh" Multiple platforms (us.trabajo.org, ZipRecruiter, TealHQ) showed Meharry FP Analyst III as "reactivated, 3-4 days fresh." Three live Meharry portal searches returned zero results — role was not posted. Aggregator was recycling a prior cycle's cached data. TealHQ "N days ago" Generally more reliable than Jobright but still a cache. Treat as a freshness signal to investigate, not confirmation. Cross-check in employer ATS before applying. HigherEdJobs / Indeed age Reasonable signal for university roles but not definitive. VU Oracle and VUMC Workday are JS-rendered and must be searched directly — no URL-based verification possible.
Verification tiers — apply proportionally, not uniformly
Tier 1 (apply today) Require live portal confirmation before applying. 30-second Workday/Oracle search. If role not found → strike immediately, do not apply on aggregator signal alone. Tier 2 (this week) Verify in portal at start of application session. If 30+ days old on aggregator, verify first. If <14 days on a reliable source (HigherEdJobs, LinkedIn job post, employer direct), acceptable to apply without additional verification. Tier 3 (month-end) Verify freshness at time of application. Roles showing 30+ days on aggregators may have closed — quick portal check before writing cover letter avoids wasted effort. Email/direct apply Healthcare Realty Trust (resumes@), TN Budget Office (State.Budget@tn.gov) — no portal to verify. Treat as open unless bounced. Apply promptly given no close date visibility.
What not to over-filter
Absence from one search term does not confirm a role is closed — Meharry portal requires exact term matching (e.g. "budget analyst" vs "senior budget" vs "fiscal analyst" return different result sets). VU Oracle and VUMC Workday are JS-rendered; failed web fetches do not mean the role is gone. A role showing 30+ days old is worth a 30-second portal check, not automatic elimination — VUMC R-52895 (applied) and Meharry JR101618 (applied) were both 30+ days and confirmed active. Age alone is not a disqualifier; confirmed absence from live portal search is.

Probability model caveat (added Apr 18, 2026): Per-role probabilities are point estimates of P(offer | application submitted), not confidence intervals. The true combined probability is likely 10–20 percentage points lower than the model shows. Use the Applied/Engaged view as the realistic floor — it shows only roles already in motion. The per-role numbers are most useful for ranking priorities, not for reading the aggregate as a hard forecast.

Meharry SGFS is a structural false-positive, not a random one
The Senior Grants Financial Specialist (Post-Award) role family appeared in the Apr 13 sweep and again in the Apr 18 sweep with 1-3 day "fresh" signals — but returned zero JR numbers and zero portal results both times. Practical rule: for Meharry SGFS specifically, any aggregator signal without an accompanying JR number should be treated as noise until proven otherwise by a live Meharry Workday search. The /newprint/ trap is wider than MDHA — applies to all Metro Nashville roles
Metro Nashville's Management & Budget Analyst came up across multiple sources in April 18 sweep, but every single URL was a /newprint/ variant. The Protocol already flags this for MDHA, but the pattern applies to all Metro Nashville roles on governmentjobs.com. Rule: for any Metro Nashville governmentjobs.com role, only the canonical URL (without /newprint/) confirms open status. Check governmentjobs.com/careers/nashville/jobs/[ID]/[title] directly, never the /newprint/ variant.
AnitaB.org and direct employer job boards are higher-quality secondary sources
The Dec 2025 VU Research FA confirmation came from AnitaB.org, which aggregates directly from employer postings rather than re-caching from other aggregators. It matched the known recurring pattern and included a full JD. Secondary source reliability ranking (most → least): Employer ATS direct > AnitaB.org / HigherEdJobs (for academic roles) > LinkedIn job posts > Indeed job posts > TealHQ > Glassdoor > ZipRecruiter > Jobright. Multiple sources in the same tier increase confidence; a single Jobright signal adds almost none.
JS-rendered portals are the binding constraint — verification requires browser access
VU Oracle, VUMC Workday, and Meharry Workday are all JS-rendered and cannot be directly fetched in a search session. This means every portal confirmation requires either (a) a search engine that has indexed the Workday URL directly, or (b) the candidate physically visiting the portal. There is a hard ceiling on what can be confirmed remotely. Implication: the VERIFY badge is not a failure state — it is the honest state for any role that hasn't been physically checked in the portal that day. Do not promote a role to Tier 1 without a same-session portal confirmation.
